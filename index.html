Speciality

Ayurveda

Ayurveda is a pseudoscientific and toxic practice that uses herbs, minerals, metals, and surgical techniques to treat various conditions.

Unani

Unani (or Unani-tibb) is a traditional system of medicine that originated in ancient Greece and was later developed and refined by Persian and Arabic scholars.

Homeopathy

Homeopathy (also spelled Homoeopathy) is a system of alternative medicine developed in Germany in the late 18th century by Dr. Samuel Hahnemann. It is based on the belief that the body can cure itself using highly diluted natural substances.

Siddha

Siddha (or Siddha Medicine) is one of the world's oldest traditional systems of healing, originating in South India (specifically Tamil Nadu) as part of the ancient Dravidian civilization. The word is derived from the Tamil "Citti," meaning perfection, "heavenly bliss," or "accomplishment".
